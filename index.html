Nerfies: Deformable Neural Radiance Fields

Yifei Zeng1, Hao Zhu2, Xun Cao2,
1Xidian University, 2Nanjing University (The work is done during Yifei Zeng makes his graduate design in Nanjing University)
Paper arXiv Video Code Data

AvatarBooth is a text-2-3D model. It creates an animatable 3D model with your word description. Also, it can generate customized model with 4~6 photos from your phone or a character design generated from diffusion model. You can play with any magic words to change your final result.

Abstract

We present a novel method for generating personalized avatars from text descriptions, utilizing as few as 4~6 personal photos or a naive character design. It can also produce vivid 3D Avatar with some word description as a Text-to-3D model.

Our approach leverages recent advancements in personalizing diffusion models and text-to-3D techniques. We address the issue of overfitting and inconsistent mesh geometry and visual results in existing finetune methods, such as LoRA or DreamBooth, by adapting ControlNet to produce multi-view photos with similar identity. This allows us to train a personalized text-to-3D Avatar model in a single stage. Our method employs a neural implicit field and a distillation loss to generate avatars with high-quality geometry and texture, through a simple yet effective pipeline.

We demonstrate that our AvatarBooth can transform casually captured photos into 3D models that are suitable for animation. Furthermore, our approach outperforms existing text-to-3D methods, achieving superior results in terms of avatar quality and fidelity to the input text description. Our method represents a significant advancement in generating personalized avatars from text, offering a promising solution for applications in virtual reality, gaming, and entertainment industries.

Video

Text-to-Avatar

Using AvatarBooth you can create high-quality avatar through a few words. The model both have a satisfying appearance and a detailed mesh.

Character Personalization

Another AvatarBooth application is that you can create your own model using your personal photos. You can add any accessories or effect to your model output using simply a word description.

Character Design

As a byproduct of our method, we can also leverage the character design generated from Stable Diffusion to create 3D model. You can keep modifying your prompt until getting a favourate character design, and then use AvatarBooth to turn it into 3D!

Application

FBX animation

We can also animate the model by turning it into a FBX file. Feel free to play with the model. You can also import the output model into Mixamo to rig it with endless motions.


Animation with Diffusion

Using the trained DreamBooth model, you can re-render the animated video with Stable Diffusion. You can now leverage the magic of T2I diffusion model to produce video of different styles.

Related Links

There's a lot of excellent work that was introduced around the same time as ours.

Progressive Encoding for Neural Optimization introduces an idea similar to our windowed position encoding for coarse-to-fine optimization.

D-NeRF and NR-NeRF both use deformation fields to model non-rigid scenes.

Some works model videos with a NeRF by directly modulating the density, such as Video-NeRF, NSFF, and DyNeRF

There are probably many more by the time you are reading this. Check out Frank Dellart's survey on recent NeRF papers, and Yen-Chen Lin's curated list of NeRF papers.
